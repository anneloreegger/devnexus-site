The Devnexus 2020 call for proposals:

OPENS July 1st 2019!

> Read the DevNexus Code Of Conduct

When and Where is the conference?

  • The conference will be in Atlanta, GA at the Georgia World Congress Center on Feb 19-21. PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS IS A DIFFERENT VENUE FROM THE 2019 EVENT
  • Workshop day is Feb 19.
  • Main conference days are Feb 20,21 .

When will I know if I'm accepted?

  • Notifications for acceptance will go out on a rolling basis; submit early and often!
  • Rejection notices will go out on October 15 or earlier.

How do I submit a session?

  • Register on the CFP site
  • Sign In, Click 'Create New Event'.
  • Make sure you also mark your availability.

What types of sessions are you looking for?

  • Workshops are full-day (9 - 5), on Feb 19
  • Presentations (lectures) are 50 mins + 5 min for questions.
  • Longer, deep dive sessions, are given priority.
  • Break apart longer sessions into multiple presentations PARTS I, II, etc.
  • A mix of skill levels (Beginners, Seasoned, Expert)
  • Select the track accordingly in the CFP system: {% for item in page.cfp_tracks %} {% endfor %}

Will you cover travel and / or lodging?

  • As we are a low-cost community conference, we have limited resources for covering presenter travel. Please ask your company if they can cover your travel first.
    • Please mark accordingly if need travel reimbursement (see below).
    • DO NOT CHANGE YOUR "COVER TRAVEL" SELECTION ONCE YOUR SESSION HAS BEEN ACCEPTED! WE GET A NOTIFICATION WHEN THIS FLAG HAS BEEN FLIPPED, AND WE WILL RE-EVALUATE YOUR ACCEPTANCE!
  • If you need to change this flag, please email us first!
