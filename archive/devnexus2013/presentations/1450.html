Google Apps Script

Track: Cloud
Skill Level: Intermediate
Room: Room 103
Time Slot: Mon 2/18, 10:30 AM
Abstract

Google Apps Script is a cloud based JavaScript environment which allows you to write powerful applications and workflows around Google Apps such as Gmail, Docs, Drive, Sheets and more. The development is entirely within a browser and its free and easy to get started. Come learn all the capabilities of Google Apps Script and walk away being able to write your first script.

Arun Nagarajan

Arun is a Senior Developer Advocate on the Google Apps Developer Relations team. Arun works closely with Google Apps customers, partners and independent developers to help build compelling applications and workflows using Google Apps Script. Prior to Google, Arun spent over 9 years at a Boston area mobile software company building a cross-platform mobile application development framework for large enterprises.
