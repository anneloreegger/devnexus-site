Please join us for live music by Delphinium Blues & Bird Dog Jubilee during the DevNexus 2016 Happy Hour!

Delphinium Blues

Delphinium Blues is a three piece folk and americana band from Atlanta, Georgia. Members include lead vocalist Lindsay Hawkins, guitarist Noah Wilson, and Leevi Palmer on mandolin. Their musical influences range from The Animals and Fleetwood Mac, to CCR and Johnny Cash.

Website: https://www.facebook.com/Delphinium-Blues-726220400818809/

Bird Dog Jubilee

Bird Dog Jubilee is a southern jam band from Atlanta, Georgia, with a sound that blends classic and psychadelic rock with a touch of the blues. Formed in early 2014 by lead guitarist RJ Fyfe and Blake Snodgrass on keys, they have musical influences ranging from The Rolling Stones and Grateful Dead to Phish.

https://www.instagram.com/bird_dog_jubilee/
