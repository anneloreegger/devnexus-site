Maps over Backlogs: User Story Mapping to share the Big Picture
A flat backlog presents problems understanding context of individual items. Determining the value of a user story in a vacuum is difficult, if not impossible. We need the big picture! User story mapping is a technique that can help us keep the big picture front and center.
