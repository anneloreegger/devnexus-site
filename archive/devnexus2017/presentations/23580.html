Demystifying Progressive Web Apps

Track: Web
Skill Level: Beginner
Room: Room A313
Time Slot: Thu 2/23, 1:00 PM
Tags: progressive web applications
Presentation Link
Abstract

Gartner recently estimated that 20% of companies will give up on their mobile apps by 2019. As users are getting more hesitant to install new apps on their devices, it is becoming more challenging and expensive to get users to find and use your app.

In this talk, you will learn what Progressive Web Applications are, and how you can build apps on the web platform that are as engaging as native apps, but as easily accessible as web pages. Best of all, these are things you can already do in your existing app.

Marcus Hellberg

Marcus is a developer advocate at Vaadin, helping find, get started, and enjoy development with Vaadin. He has a background in embedded systems but has worked on everything web related for the past 11+ years.
