Biography

Joe has been in the Software Industry for over 20 years. He has successfully designed, built and delivered enterprise grade software in the financial services and health sector in the Toronto Canada area. He has been using Spring for over 10 years and has recently joined the Spring Security engineering team, in early 2016. Outside of his passion for crafty software, Joe continues to travel the world with his family, snowboarding the most challenging mountains, exploring nature on foot and doing his best to enjoy what life brings.
