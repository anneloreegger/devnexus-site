What You Need to Know About Open Source

Track: Practices and Other Tech
Abstract

Come learn what open source really means and investigate the most popular OSS licenses in use today—from a lawyer’s perspective. We all use open source in our projects. It is important to understand the impact it has on our work and our organizations.

Jeff Strauss

Jeff is a software developer, an entrepreneur, and an attorney. He maintains a passion for solving problems with inspired solutions, improving life through technology. Beyond speaking, Jeff is a conference organizer, helping to run the Kansas City Developer Conference.
