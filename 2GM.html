2GM at DevNexus

Devnexus teams up with 2GM

For its 2019 conference, Devnexus has teamed up with the 2GM (Groovy, Grails, and Micronaut) communities, in furtherance of its mission, to promote and support open source values and technologies.

We invite everyone in the 2GM communities to submit their proposals for workshops, presentations, and deep-dive sessions for the new 2GM Track. The deadline to submit proposals is rapidly approaching, so if you’ve got a Groovy, Grails, or Micronaut talk you’d like to present, the clock is ticking!

How do I submit a session?

  • Register on the CFP site
  • Sign In, Click 'Create New Event'.
  • Select the 2GM Track in the CFP System
  • Mark your availability.

What types of sessions are you looking for?

  • Presentations are 50 mins + 5 min for questions.
  • Longer, deep dive sessions, are given priority.
  • Break apart longer sessions into multiple presentations PARTS I, II, etc.
  • A mix of skill levels (Beginners, Seasoned, Expert)
  • Select the 2GM track in the CFP system when you submit!
  • Need some inspiration? Check out these upcoming Micronaut talks.

When will I know if I'm accepted?

  • Notifications for acceptance will go out on a rolling basis; submit early and often!
