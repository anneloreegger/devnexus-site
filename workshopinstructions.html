Download and Install Early

In order to maximize your learning time, setup before you arrive on Wednesday. The conference wi-fi is a public network and we cannot guarantee speed while downloading all of maven, github and npm.

We cannot guarantee assistance with specific install / setup issues on the day of the workshop. However, we do have an active and helpful community. Please post your questions early, and more than likely someone will be able to help.
